# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Analytics stakeholders, business decision-makers, and data consumers who need to quickly understand key metrics, trends, and performance indicators. They access this from modern devices (desktop-first) to monitor health of business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

## Product Purpose

A modern analytics dashboard that surfaces key business metrics, trends, and conversations in a way that feels premium and trustworthy. It replaces outdated admin tools with a dashboard that stakeholders *want* to use—one that's clean, fast, and communicates confidence through design.

## Brand Personality

Modern, sleek, premium. Like a fintech product that respects the user's time and intelligence. Sophisticated without being cold. High-quality without being fussy.

## Anti-references

- Generic admin templates with dark sidebars, grid-of-identical-cards, or flat hierarchy
- Dated corporate-looking tools (2000s enterprise software vibes)
- Overstuffed dashboards that overwhelm with data instead of highlighting signal

## Design Principles

- **Clarity over density**: Show what matters most. Use space and hierarchy to reduce cognitive load.
- **Premium through craft**: Micro-interactions, smooth animations, careful typography. Design that feels intentional.
- **Data should feel human**: Metrics are about people and business outcomes. Visualizations should tell a story, not just display numbers.
- **Modern confidence**: Light, airy, high-contrast. Avoid the dark/neon aesthetic that screams "tech tool."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA standard. Ensure all metrics and charts are readable without relying on color alone (colorblind users). Respect reduced motion preferences. Text contrast must be strong (≥4.5:1 for body text, ≥3:1 for large text).
